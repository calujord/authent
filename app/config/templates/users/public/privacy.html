database Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Registration Data

  • person Full name, email address, phone number
  • local_hospital Clinic name, professional license number
  • badge Professional role (dentist, assistant, administrator)
  • receipt_long Fiscal/billing information

2.2 Patient Medical Data

  • child_care Patient name, date of birth, medical history
  • medical_information Dental treatment records and diagnoses
  • medication Prescriptions and medication records
  • monitor_heart Health evaluations and clinical notes

2.3 Images and Camera

Our app requests camera permission to allow dental professionals to:

  • photo_camera Capture clinical documents of patients for treatment records
  • panorama Photograph panoramic X-rays/radiographs for AI-powered dental analysis
  • add_a_photo Upload profile photos for user accounts

All images captured are stored securely and associated with the corresponding patient record. Images of radiographs may be processed by our AI system to assist in business analytics. Camera access is only used when explicitly initiated by the user.

2.4 Location Data

  • location_on Clinic address for registration and service purposes
  • my_location Approximate location may be inferred from IP address for security and fraud prevention

2.5 Device and Technical Data

  • devices Device type, operating system, app version
  • wifi IP address, browser information
  • analytics App usage patterns and crash reports
